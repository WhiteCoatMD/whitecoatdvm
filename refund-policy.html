Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2024

At WhiteCoat DVM, we strive to provide exceptional veterinary telemedicine services. We want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. Please review our refund policy carefully before subscribing.

24-Hour Satisfaction Guarantee: If you are not satisfied with our service, you may request a full refund within 24 hours of your initial subscription purchase.

Refund Eligibility

Full Refunds

You are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:

  • New Subscriptions: You may request a full refund within 24 hours of your initial subscription purchase
  • Technical Issues: If we are unable to provide the service due to technical problems on our end
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly due to a system error
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were accidentally charged more than once for the same subscription period

No Refunds After 24 Hours

After the initial 24-hour satisfaction guarantee period, subscription fees are non-refundable. This includes:

  • Monthly subscriptions after 24 hours from initial purchase
  • 90-day, 6-month, and annual subscriptions after 24 hours from initial purchase
  • Renewal charges for any subscription plan
  • Partial refunds for unused portions of subscription periods

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 24-hour satisfaction guarantee period:

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@whitecoatdvm.com
  2. Include your account email address and subscription details
  3. Briefly explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of your initial purchase

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges. Please note:

  • Cancellations prevent future billing cycles but do not provide refunds for the current subscription period
  • You will retain access to WhiteCoat DVM services until the end of your current paid subscription period
  • To cancel, log into your account and visit the billing settings, or contact customer support

First Month Promotional Pricing

Our first month promotional pricing of $5 for the monthly plan is subject to the same 24-hour refund policy. After 24 hours, the $5 promotional fee is non-refundable.

Exceptions and Special Circumstances

WhiteCoat DVM reserves the right to evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis for exceptional circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Extended service outages or disruptions
  • Medical or family emergencies
  • Other extraordinary situations at our discretion

Auto-Renewal Policy

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle. You will be notified before each renewal. To avoid being charged for the next billing cycle, you must cancel your subscription before the renewal date. Renewal charges are not eligible for refunds.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact us:

Email: support@whitecoatdvm.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24 hours

Changes to This Policy

WhiteCoat DVM reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
