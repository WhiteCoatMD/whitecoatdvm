Frequently Asked Questions

How does TeleVet work?

Connect instantly with licensed veterinarians through our on-demand platform - no appointments necessary. Just log in and get professional guidance for your pet's health concerns immediately.

What types of issues can be addressed through TeleVet?

Our veterinarians can help with behavioral concerns, minor health issues, medication questions, wellness advice, and determining if in-person care is needed. Emergency situations require immediate in-person veterinary care.

Are the veterinarians licensed?

Yes, all WhiteCoat DVM veterinarians are fully licensed professionals with years of experience in veterinary medicine and telemedicine consultations.

How many pets are covered under one subscription?

Each monthly subscription covers up to 6 pets in your household with unlimited consultations for just $24.99 per month.

What if my pet needs emergency care?

TeleVet is not suitable for emergency situations. If your pet is experiencing a medical emergency, please contact your local veterinary emergency clinic immediately.

Can veterinarians prescribe medications through TeleVet?

Prescription capabilities vary by state regulations. Our veterinarians will inform you of what's possible in your location and can provide guidance on obtaining necessary medications.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Simply log into your WhiteCoat DVM account and connect instantly with a licensed veterinarian - no scheduling required, available on-demand 24/7.

Is there a free trial?

Your first month is only $5! Experience our full service for just $5 in your first month, then continue at regular pricing.
