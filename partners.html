Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any cost to become a partner?

No. The partnership is completely free. There are no signup fees, monthly fees, or hidden costs.

How do I get paid?

We send payments monthly via check or direct deposit. You earn $10 for every active subscriber referred through your link each month.

What does WhiteCoat DVM offer my adopters?

Unlimited 24/7 virtual veterinary consultations with licensed vets for $20/month, covering up to 6 pets per household. Perfect for those late-night "is my pet okay?" moments.

How long do I keep earning from a referral?

As long as the subscriber remains active, you earn $10/month. Many subscribers stay for years, creating long-term recurring revenue.

What promotional materials do you provide?

We provide social media graphics, email templates, website banners, printable flyers for adoption packets, and a unique referral link to track your subscribers.

Can I include this in our adoption packets?

Absolutely! We'll send you printable inserts perfect for adoption folders. Many of our most successful partners include WhiteCoat DVM info with every adoption.

How quickly can we get started?

Once you fill out the partner form, we'll have your referral link and materials ready within 24 hours.
